Other Peoples Stuff — Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: 2025-09-18

How to request deletion

Email us from the Google account used in the app and include your in-app name (if set):

Email deletion request

You can also request deletion of specific items (posts/photos/comments) without closing your account.

What we delete

  • Account identifiers (Google account ID, display name, email).
  • User-generated content (posts, photos, comments, favorites).
  • In-app activity linked to your account (likes, bookmarks).

What may be retained

  • Minimal server logs and abuse-prevention records for up to 90 days.
  • Data kept to meet legal obligations or resolve disputes, if required.
  • Aggregated, non-identifiable analytics.

Timeline

We begin processing within 72 hours and complete deletion within 30 days (unless a longer period is required by law).

Verification

We may ask you to verify the request from the same Google account used to sign in.
