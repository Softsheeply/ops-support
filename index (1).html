Request account deletion

If you’d like to permanently delete your account and associated data for Other Peoples Stuff, email us using the button below. You don’t need to be signed in to make this request.

Email deletion request

We’ll verify ownership using your registered email address and then proceed with deletion.

What gets deleted

  • Profile data (name, email, avatar).
  • Your posts, comments, likes, and saved items.
  • App preferences stored on our servers.

Limited records may be retained where legally required (e.g., payment receipts if you ever purchased add-ons). These are not used for any other purpose and are purged once no longer required.

Data access / correction

You can request a copy of your data or ask us to correct it by emailing softsheepapps@gmail.com.

Encryption in transit

All data sent between the app and our servers is protected with HTTPS (TLS).
